04 · Profile breakdown

Instagram audit

Profile photo

Your current photo reads more like a headshot than a trainer at work. An action shot with weights or a gym behind you signals a top of the line fitness professional in the first few seconds.

Bio and name

Your name is not on the page anywhere. Adding "Coach Natalie" with "Online Fitness Coach" puts a face on the brand, and saying online raises your perceived value. A direct "I help" statement then tells the right woman she is in the right place.

Content and reels

Everything you post is your marketing. When someone lands on your page they decide in two to three seconds whether to stay. Your content needs to teach and speak to what your ideal client wants, so the right woman sees you as the expert and reaches out.

06 · The Blueprint

Strategy preview Steal these hooks and topics using the prompt in section 08.

Messaging direction

Strength for endurance. You're the coach for driven women 40 to 60 who love to train and want to stay strong, fast, and healthy for years, with strength work built around their sport.

The comeback after injury. You speak to women rebuilding after an injury or surgery and show them a clear, safe path back to full strength.

The videos are scripted for you. For your first eight weeks, Fit Boss researches what is working in your niche, writes each video word for word, and pairs it with the format to film. You film it, edit it, and post it. The three topics below are examples of what those scripts cover.

Content topics

Strength training for endurance athletes

Endurance athletes often chase more miles and skip the strength work that protects them. This shows why strength is what keeps them racing.

Coming back to training after an injury

Many women pause training after an injury and lose ground. This shows how to rebuild safely instead of starting over.

Building a training structure that fits a full schedule

Driven women with packed schedules run on willpower until it breaks. This shows how a clear structure carries the work when motivation dips.

Hook preview

Steal these hooks with the prompt in section 08
Attract
I have been a (occupation) for (time) and I am exposing everything the (industry) keeps from you.
Example: I have been a personal trainer for 26 years and I am exposing everything the fitness industry keeps from you.
Convert
If you're (age range), these are the (number) things you need to do so you don't end up (pain point) by (age).
Example: If you're a woman in your 40s or 50s, these are the 3 things you need to do so you don't lose your strength by 60.
Enroll
Over the past (time) my client went from (before) to (after).
Example: Over the past 12 weeks my client went from afraid to load her legs again to lifting heavier than before her injury.
