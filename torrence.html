04 · Profile breakdown

Instagram audit

Profile photo

Your photo works. You are on brand, looking at the camera, and smiling. Keep it.

Bio and name

The name field is just your name. Adding "Online Fitness Coach" after it tells a stranger who you are and what you do in the three seconds you get, and it puts you in Instagram search when someone types "online fitness coach." Under it, a direct "I help" statement names who you serve and the result you deliver.

Content and reels

Your content shows that you are in shape. It does not yet show that you coach. A stranger scrolling your page can see you know how to train yourself, and still has no reason to believe they can come to you for help. Coaching in your content is the fix.

One page, one focus

Your page also reads as a local gym. When somebody outside Illinois lands on it, they count themselves out. Treat the personal page as the online coaching page, and let Synergy Fitness LLC carry the brick and mortar location.

05 · Direction

What to change

Name and bio

Make it clear in three seconds who you help and how.

  • Coach Torrence | Online Fitness Coach
  • Add an "I help" statement: I help busy professionals lose weight by taking the thinking off their plate
Why: the right person knows in one glance that they are in the right place.
One page for online, one page for the gym

Point each account at one job.

  • Personal page carries the online coaching
  • Synergy Fitness LLC carries the brick and mortar location
Why: a confused viewer makes no decision at all.
Coach in your content

Show the work, not just the result.

  • Build the 45 minute workout on camera
  • Fix an exercise people get wrong
  • Explain what to do with bands, a few dumbbells, and body weight
Why: people hire the coach who has already coached them.
Send the room to the page

You get into hundreds of conversations a week in the gym. Give every one of them somewhere to go.

  • Point them to your Instagram, not just the gym
  • Film the questions people ask you and post the answers
Why: the conversations you already have turn into followers, DMs, and online clients.
06 · The Blueprint

Strategy preview Steal these hooks and topics using the prompt in section 08.

Messaging direction

Take the thinking off their plate. You coach people who are already busy and already stretched. You build the program, you pick the exercises, you tell them what to do in the 45 minutes they have. They show up and train.

Train for the life you actually live. Lunging, picking things up off the ground, carrying luggage and groceries. You coach people so their body holds up in the day they already have, at home, with bands, body weight, and a few dumbbells.

The videos are scripted for you. For your first eight weeks, Fit Boss researches what is working in your niche, writes each video word for word, and pairs it with the format to film. You film it, edit it, and post it. The three topics below are examples of what those scripts cover.

Content topics

How to put together a 45 minute workout with bands, dumbbells, and body weight

Your people have the time slot and the gear, and no idea what to do with it. This shows them how a real workout gets built from very little.

What functional training actually means in a normal day

Most people hear "functional" and picture nothing. Naming it as lunging, lifting, and carrying shows them that training is for the day they live, not for the gym.

How to train consistently around a full schedule

The people you want to serve have a time problem. This helps them see that consistency is built around their schedule instead of squeezed into whatever is left over.
